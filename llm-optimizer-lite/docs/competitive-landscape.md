# Competitive Landscape

## TensorZero
Powerful and enterprise-oriented, but heavier and Rust-centric for many small teams.

## LiteLLM
Great unified model access and proxy patterns; less focused on a built-in optimization loop.

## Langfuse
Strong observability and tracing; broader platform scope than this lightweight toolkit.

## Agenta
Strong experimentation workflows with broader platform patterns.

## OptiLLM
Useful optimization proxy framing; LLM Optimizer Lite focuses on transparent Python-first extensibility.
