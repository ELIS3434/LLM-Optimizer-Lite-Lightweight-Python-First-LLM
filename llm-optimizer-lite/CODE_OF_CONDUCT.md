# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, abuse, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private data without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributors that violate this code.
